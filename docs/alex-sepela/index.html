Writing samples

Pull content into a module

An explanation and reference document for platform admins. In contrast, the internal configuration documentation (not shown here) includes many more technical details.
Plan a multi-site platform

An explanation document for platform admins. It connects key concepts that organizers should understand if they plan to create multiple websites within one platform.
Enroll, unenroll, and waitlist learners

A how-to document for course managers. It describes how to do enrollment tasks and explains the prerequisites that affect how enrollment works.
Video privacy settings

A reference document for platform admins. It lists the privacy settings required for videos hosted on external platforms. Getting these settings wrong can interrupt a live event, so what not to do is emphasized just as much as what to do.
Take a course

A tutorial for online learners. It walks them through the process of taking and submitting a course. Understanding this process ahead of time is important to learners who are nervous about making mistakes during a graded course that impacts their degree or certification.
