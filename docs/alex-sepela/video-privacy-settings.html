Video privacy settings

This document was originally published on help.junolive.com.

When you add a video to your site, the privacy settings in your Vimeo, YouTube, Wistia, or Brightcove account matter. In general, if videos are set to Private, attendees can‚Äôt watch them on your JUNO site. This article lists our recommended privacy settings.

A note about regional laws

Many video services, including Vimeo and YouTube, are blocked in China. This means that any content hosted on or embedded from those platforms is not visible to users in China.

In general, we recommend confirming with your video service provider that all features and functionality will work as expected in your audience‚Äôs regions.
